A Google Groups Material Design revamp that’s been in beta for several months will become the default for all users starting next week, the company announced in a blog post. The new interface, which has several “highly requested features,” according to Google, includes a new mobile design, updated collaborative inboxes, and replaces tags and categories in groups with labels. The mobile redesign of Groups will do away with the old and surprisingly outdated look and give it an appearance closer to the desktop version.
